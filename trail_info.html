Trail Info, Safety Guidelines + FAQs

Know before you go—ride smart and enjoy the Indian Creek Trail

A signed waiver is required before any rental begins.

Trail Info

The Indian Creek Trail is a popular paved multi-use path used by cyclists, walkers, runners, and families. Expect occasional busy sections—especially on weekends and nice weather days.

Surface:
Paved multi-use trail
Best Times:
Early mornings / weekdays for lighter traffic
What to Bring:
Water, sunscreen, and a charged phone

Safety Guidelines

Wear a helmet (recommended for all riders)
Ride on the right, pass on the left
Announce “on your left” before passing
Obey all posted trail signs + crossings
Keep a safe speed in crowded areas
Use lights/reflectors if riding near dusk

Please be respectful of others on the trail. Slow down around pedestrians, give extra space to kids and pets, and avoid sudden stops in the middle of the path.

Waiver Required

All riders must review and sign a liability waiver before renting a bike. Riders under 18 must have a parent or guardian sign for them.

FAQs

Do I need a helmet?

Helmets are strongly recommended for all riders, especially kids.

Can I ride with kids?

Yes—just keep speeds comfortable and stay to the right. Kids should ride with an adult.

What if the weather is bad?

If conditions are unsafe (ice, storms, heavy rain), we recommend rescheduling your ride.

Can I bring my dog?

Dogs are welcome on most trails when leashed. Please keep them close to the right side.

What should I do if something goes wrong?

Move off the trail, check for injuries, and contact us if your rental needs assistance.

Can I stop for breaks?

Absolutely—just pull completely off the trail so others can pass safely.
