How to Measure

1

Measure Your Height

Stand against a wall without shoes. Mark the top of your head and measure from the floor to the mark.

2

Check Inseam (Optional)

Measure from your crotch to the floor. This helps determine standover height clearance.

3

Match to Size Chart

Find your height in the chart above to determine the recommended wheel size.

4

Test Ride if Possible

The best way to ensure a good fit is to test ride the bike before renting.

Sizing Tips

When in doubt, go smaller. It's easier to adjust a slightly smaller bike than one that's too large.

Consider growth for kids. Kids grow quickly. A slightly larger bike with room to grow can last longer.

Standover height matters. You should be able to stand over the bike with both feet flat on the ground.

Reach should be comfortable. You shouldn't feel stretched out or cramped when holding the handlebars.
