Our Story

Indian Creek Cycles began in 2010 with a simple mission: to share the joy of cycling with everyone. What started as a small bike repair shop has grown into the region's premier bike rental destination.

Founded by lifelong cycling enthusiasts Mike and Sarah Thompson, our business was born from a passion for two-wheeled adventure and a desire to make quality bikes accessible to all.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of families, couples, and solo adventurers discover the beauty of our local trails and create memories that last a lifetime.

Our Mission & Values

Passion for Cycling

We believe cycling is more than transportation—it's a way to connect with nature, stay healthy, and create lasting memories with loved ones.

Quality & Safety

Every bike in our fleet is meticulously maintained and safety-checked before each rental. Your safety is our top priority.

Family Friendly

We welcome riders of all ages and skill levels. From training wheels to high-end mountain bikes, we have something for everyone.

Environmental Stewardship

We're committed to preserving the natural beauty of our trails. We practice and promote responsible, eco-friendly cycling.
