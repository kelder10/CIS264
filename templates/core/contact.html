Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring to rent a bike?

You'll need a valid ID and a credit card for the security deposit. We provide helmets with all rentals, but you're welcome to bring your own.

Can I reserve a bike in advance?

Yes! We recommend booking online at least 24 hours in advance, especially on weekends and holidays. You can reserve through our website or by calling us.

What if it rains on my rental day?

We understand that weather can be unpredictable. You can reschedule your rental up to 2 hours before your pickup time at no extra charge.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer discounts for groups of 4 or more. Contact us for special rates for corporate events, school groups, or large family outings.

Are your bikes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We have bikes for all skill levels. Our staff will help you choose the right bike and can recommend beginner-friendly trails.
