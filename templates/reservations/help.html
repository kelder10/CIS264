03. One-Touch Unlock & Return

  1. Locate & Scan: Find your reserved bike and scan the QR code on the Smart-Dock for an instant release.
  2. Digital Dashboard: Alternatively, use our app interface to remotely unlock gear, check real-time dock availability, or manage multiple reservations.
  3. Go & Return: Once your ride is complete, you can return your bike to any available Smart-Dock on the trail or bring it back to the ICC Hub.
Flexible Returns: Check the app to see which stations have open docks before you arrive to ensure a smooth drop-off.

Hardware Tip: Look for flashing green lights on the dock!
