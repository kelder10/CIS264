# Indian Creek Cycle Rentals

## Executive Summary

Indian Creek Bicycle Rentals provides convenient and family-friendly bicycle rentals along the Indian Creek Trail, a popular paved trail system used by walkers, runners, and cyclists of all ages. Located near key trailheads and parks, the business offers a variety of bicycle styles designed to meet the needs of different riders, including children, casual riders, fitness enthusiasts, and families.

The company targets both local residents and visitors looking for a fun, healthy, and accessible outdoor activity without needing to bring their own bikes. Rental options include children’s bikes, standard adult bikes, comfort cruisers, hybrid/fitness bikes, and electric-assist bicycles. Each bike type has different pricing based on features, size, and maintenance costs.

Indian Creek Bicycle Rentals is a new venture focused on testing demand along a high-traffic trail corridor near cafés, playgrounds, and neighborhood shopping areas. The digital platform will allow customers to browse bike options, reserve bikes online, and select rental durations while highlighting the scenic nature of the trail.

Key challenges include managing inventory across multiple bike types and sizes, preventing double-bookings, handling age or height restrictions for certain bikes, and adjusting pricing during peak usage times such as weekends and holidays.

---

## Objectives

- Develop an online bicycle rental system with support for multiple bike types and sizes.
- Offer tiered pricing based on bicycle style and rental duration.
- Provide an easy-to-use reservation experience for individuals and families.
- Prevent overbooking by tracking real-time bike availability.
- Enable secure online payments.
- Provide administrators with tools to manage inventory, pricing, and maintenance status.
- Collect data on usage patterns, revenue, and customer preferences.

---

## Benefits of the System

- Simplifies the rental process by clearly presenting bike options for different age groups and riding styles.
- Reduces scheduling conflicts by tracking inventory in real time.
- Expands customer reach by offering bikes for children, adults, and seniors.
- Increases revenue through premium pricing for specialty bikes (e.g., electric bikes).
- Improves customer satisfaction with transparent availability and pricing.
- Encourages outdoor recreation and supports the local trail community.

## Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Requirements

### User Management

- Customer accounts with booking history.
- Administrator accounts with permissions to manage bikes and pricing.

### Reservation System

- Online booking with date and time selection.
- Bicycle selection by type and size.
- Rental duration options (hourly, half-day, full-day).
- Automated reservation confirmations and reminders.

### Bicycle Inventory Management

- Separate inventory tracking for each bike type:
  - Children’s bikes
  - Adult standard bikes
  - Comfort cruisers
  - Hybrid/fitness bikes
  - Electric-assist bikes
- Bike availability updates in real time.
- Alerts to prevent overbooking.

### Pricing & Payments

- Tiered pricing based on bike type and rental duration.
- Secure online payment processing.
- Digital receipts and invoices.

### Marketing & Content

- Photos of each bicycle type in use on the trail.
- Trail maps and nearby points of interest.
- Safety and riding guidelines.

---

## User Interface Hints

### For Customers

- Visual bike selector with images and descriptions.
- Filters for bike type, rider age, and rental duration.
- Clear pricing breakdown by bike category.
- Trail information and suggested ride distances.

### For Administrators

- Dashboard showing available bikes by type and size.
- Tools to update pricing and mark bikes as unavailable for maintenance.
- Reports on usage and revenue by bike category.

---

## Notifications

- Booking confirmation emails or texts.
- Rental start and return reminders.
- Weather advisories or closure notifications.
- Alerts for overdue bike returns.

---

## Key Metrics to Capture

- Number of rentals by bike type.
- Utilization rates for each bicycle category.
- Revenue by bike type and rental duration.
- Peak rental times (weekday vs. weekend).
- Customer satisfaction ratings and reviews.

---

## Critical Thinking Questions

- How should pricing differ between standard bikes and electric-assist bikes?
- Should discounts be offered for families renting multiple bikes?
- How can the system enforce age, height, or helmet requirements?
- Should add-ons such as helmets, child seats, or locks be included or priced separately?
- How can trail usage data be used to improve bike availability and staffing?
